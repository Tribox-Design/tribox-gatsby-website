  Cebu Farmers Marker (CFM) is a non-profit organization
  based in Cebu City. This is a market that features fresh,
  organic, and naturally grown fruits and vegetables from
  local farmers in Cebu. They have operated since 2004,
  helping farmers sell their fresh produce to the community.
  As a founder of CFM, Teresa D. Ruelas advocates the
  “Farm Right, Eat Right, Live Right” campaign to Cebuanos.
